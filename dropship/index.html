1 The Complete Guide 6 steps

1

🇨🇳 Find Products on 1688

Go to 1688.com (China's wholesale marketplace — like Taobao for bulk). Use Chrome auto-translate.

  • Search for a product category (e.g. "phone case", "slippers", "toys")
  • Look for products with multiple color/size options
  • Check user reviews with photos
  • Save these details: Title, description, price, images, specs, colors/sizes
💡 Pro tip: Contact the 1688 merchant and ask "Can you do dropshipping?" Most will agree — just ask.
2

🛒 List on Shopee Method A

You need a Shopee seller account first (register in Shopee app → Become a Seller).

  • Go to Seller Center → Add New Product
  • Upload 3+ product images (from 1688)
  • Copy title & description from 1688 (translate to Malay/English)
  • Set price = cost from 1688 + shipping + your profit (30% minimum)
  • Click Save & Publish
⚠️ Shopee requires business info (tax number, business registration). Register as "Individual" seller first if you don't have a company.
3

🛒 Use CJ Dropshipping Method B (Easier)

Skip manual order handling. Use CJ Dropshipping as a middle-man between 1688 and Shopee.

  • Create a CJ Dropshipping account
  • Connect your Shopee store to CJ (Settings → Connect Store)
  • Find products in CJ's catalog (they source from 1688 for you)
  • Push products directly to your Shopee with 1 click
  • When customer orders, CJ auto-fulfills — you don't touch anything
✅ With CJ, you never handle inventory or shipping. Just focus on finding products and marketing.
4

🎵 List on TikTok Shop

TikTok Shop is HUGE right now. But it has strict rules.

  • Register at shop.tiktok.com → Choose "Individual" (no company needed)
  • Upload your ID and tax number → Wait for approval (hours to 1 day)
  • CRITICAL: TikTok requires delivery within 6 business days
🚨 1688 direct shipping is too slow for TikTok! You MUST use Amazon Prime as your fulfillment source — order from Amazon when customer buys from you.
  • Set up a shipping template: Seller Shipping → Standard (7 days) → Free shipping
  • Create your product listing with images from 1688
  • Connect to AutoDS ($1 for 30 days trial) to auto-fulfill from Amazon
💡 AutoDS automatically: detects new orders → buys from Amazon → ships to customer → updates tracking. Zero manual work.
5

🎬 Create Content (TikTok Only)

TikTok is a content platform. You NEED videos to get sales.

  • Option A: Record yourself — Order the product to your house, record unboxing/demo. No need to show face!
  • Option B: AI videos — Use tools like Create UGC. Paste your product link → AI generates videos with avatars
  • Option C: Copy what works — Find competitors' viral videos → recreate with your own product
🔥 TikTok's Affiliate Program: Creators promote your product for commission. You only pay when they sell. Free traffic!
6

💰 Fulfill Orders & Get Paid

When a customer buys from you:

  • Shopee (via CJ): CJ handles everything automatically
  • Shopee (manual): Copy customer address → send to your 1688 supplier → supplier ships → paste tracking number
  • TikTok (via AutoDS): AutoDS orders from Amazon → Amazon ships → tracking auto-updates
  • TikTok (manual): Copy customer info → order from Amazon Prime → paste tracking
⚠️ For TikTok: Always use Amazon Prime (2-day shipping) to meet TikTok's 6-day delivery requirement.

⚠️ Common Mistakes (Avoid These!)

❌ Using 1688 direct shipping for TikTok

TikTok requires delivery in 6 business days. 1688 shipping takes 10-20 days. Always use Amazon Prime as middle-man for TikTok orders.

❌ Not checking Amazon Prime availability

Before listing a product on TikTok, make sure the same product exists on Amazon with Prime shipping. No Prime = no TikTok.

❌ Setting prices too low

Your price = product cost + shipping cost + 30% minimum margin. Don't compete on price — compete on product selection and content.

❌ Posting no content on TikTok

TikTok is not Shopee. You can't just list and wait. You MUST post videos or use the affiliate program to get creators to post for you.
